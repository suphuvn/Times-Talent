About Us

 

Times Talent is a startup company dedicated to helping people and companies achieve their dreams through our professional recruiting services.

How it works is that we will provide a list of job postings from various companies across Vietnam. In each postings, there will be descriptions and application information to guide our viewers through the process. When the viewer has sent their application packages, all they have to do left is to submit and connect with their employers or job posters for further instructions.

It is fair and simple with just three easy steps.

Find, Apply, Connect!
